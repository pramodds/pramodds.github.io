Notes of Drawing

Drawing was a hobby since childhood (I wasn't so good at it). Initially I just used to try pencil sketches at which I am very bad and did not know about this kind of art.
I am a guy who is interested by literature involving simple yet effective inner meanings about life, greatly influenced by books like Ramayana, etc. I used to attend speeches at a library where I learnt about the dualities of life (such as good and bad, happy and sad, ups and downs, and so on).
The impact of this led me to think that I should represent these dualities in sketches. The most promising way that attracted me was using Black and White, which represents dualities and also life, when seen with a balanced mind as a combination of dualties, makes more meaningful.
This led me to start learning with black and white. The first sketch was done from seeing a newspaper image which involved black and orange.
The learning is a long process which I am still exploring. A moment of happiness amidst busy life can be experienced by seeing something beautiful. It is still one of my hobbies and I am continuing it not to please anyone or advertise myself but just to make people happy for atleast a second of their busy stressful life.

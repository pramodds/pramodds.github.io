About Me

Hi there.

I'm Pramod, and I work at Total Environment in Bangalore. I love to draw and blog, and have sketched more than a hundred pieces, which you can find here. I speak in English and Kannada, and love making poems in Kannada. I like reading stuff, be it an article from Quora or a full length novel. I like solving Sudoku and taking lovely photos. I like swimming too. Yet, I believe more in good character than amazing talent. I love to draw in black and white. I wanted to study mechanical as I always had a profound liking of the subject in whole.
